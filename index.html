I am the Lab Manager of the Affective Science Lab under Dr. Kristen Lindquist. My research examines how early life stress shapes the way people perceive, interpret, and narrate their own experiences. I am particularly interested in the gap between subjective experience and objective reality, and how the meaning individuals construct from adversity relates to resilience and clinical functioning.
